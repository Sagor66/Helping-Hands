About us

Helping Hands. A non-profit Organization. Our sole purpose is to help. This organization is the center point of all the charity organizations in Bangladesh. We have at least one partner in every division. These organizations are always ready to help. They are always ready to make the life of others better. We also take donation for paitent who don't have the money for treatment, children who are meritorious but don't have enough money to continue their study. We are here to help.
